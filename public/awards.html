  • Student Awards
  • Richard E. Merwin Student Scholarship
    Are you an active student volunteer leader in a student branch or chapter? If so, do not miss the chance to win $40,000 in student scholarships from $1,000 and up offered by IEEE Computer Society to reward student leadership.
  • Lance Stafford Larson Scholarship
    IEEE Computer Society has created a special award to encourage undergraduate students members to develop excellence in their communication skills and to motivate students toward achievement in CS. The winner of the award will get $500 plus a certificate of commendation. Runner ups will also get a certificate of commendation.
  • Upsilon Pi Epsilon Honor Society
    We want to invite all graduate and undergraduate students who are IEEE Computer Society members and who had minimum grade point average of 3.0 out of a possible 4.0 in your academic transcript are invited to apply for this scholarship. Up to four awards of $1,000 each are given each year to competition winners, plus a certificate of commendation, and a periodical subscription for one year.
  • Outstanding Chapter Award
    The IEEE Computer Society wants to award the CS chapter and student branch chapter that has provided their membership with the best overall set of programs and activities. If your chapter wins this award, it will be recognized for achieving excellence in its chapter operations at a Computer Society Board of Governors meeting.
