Call for Volunteers

  • Home /
  • Volunteers

Computer Society Member Development Committee for Region 8 (Europe, Africa and Middle East) is looking for amazing volunteers in order to make great things happen at Computer Society and achieve as much as possible.


If you:
- Have great ideas and want to make them come true
- Want to use your expertise and experience to help others
- Are enthusiastic about new technologies and innovations
- Have been used to working with others in a team

Then, you would belong perfectly in this Committee :)!


If you are interested in joining us, all you have to do is:

1) Make sure you are an active IEEE Computer Society member
(if you are not a member yet, you can apply here)

2) Choose what you would love to do:

CopyWriter

Responsible for writing or developing announcements, e-mails, posts, contents, logos and other materials.

Web Designer

Responsible for the maintenance of our web page and the consecutive renewal of its page contents.



Social Media Manager

Responsible for managing social media sites (Facebook, Twitter and Google+ and posting about every updates.

Member Relations Manager

Responsible for keeping track of the R8 CS members and contacting them for feedback and support.



Student Chapters Coordinator

Responsible for keeping track of all the R8 CS Student Chapters and their activities.

Section Chapters Coordinator

Responsible for keeping track of all the R8 CS Section Chapters and their activities.


3) Apply


Thank you for your interest :)!
Looking forwarding to working with you!
