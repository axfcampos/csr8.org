  • Special Technical Communities
  • Broadening Participation
    STCBP is an international Special Technical Community which aims to reference, support, highlight, and connect the many existing international initiatives for broadening participation in computing.
  • Cloud Computing
    Cloud Computing Special Technical Community’s main focus is to enable Computer Society member’s participation into Cloud activities and initiatives.
  • Education
    STC for Education is a community of those interested in research and pedagogy in the field of computing and helps them become experts in the area, through a wide variety of models ranging from action research to purely theoretical works.
  • e-Government
    e-Gov STC is an open community related to the use of the Information and Communication Technology in the context of public policy, operations in public organizations, citizen engagement or government services.

    • .

  • Haptics
    The Haptics Technical Community’s goals are to integrate the diverse interests in the field of Haptics research and to improve communication among the other fields.
  • Human Computation
    The mission of the STCHC is to provide a community home for computer scientists to engage in activities related to Human Computation and to support those activities.
  • Multicore
    The Special Technical Community on Multicore provides places for discussion to communities regarding research, development and education in the field of multicore and manycore technologies.
  • Operating Systems
    This Community promotes research and advancement of education in the areas related to the Operating Systems and Software Systems by organizing conferences and publishing newsletters, among others.

    • .

  • Smartgrid
    Computer and Smart Grid Technical Community aims to enable achievement of Smart Grid visions by stimulating investments in computing technologies.
  • Social Networking
    STCSN helps researchers communicate with like-minded people through social networking tools, such as Facebook, Twitter, Google and Linkedin.
  • Sustainable Computing
    The STC on Sustainable Computing is relevant to any domain that is using and aims to promote the design of sustainable computing and facilitate computing for sustainability.
  • Systems Engineering
    The STC-SysE goal is to advance the principles of Systems Engineering as applied to development and sustainment of computer-based and software-intensive systems.
